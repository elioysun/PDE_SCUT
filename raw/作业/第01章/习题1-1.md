# 习题 1-1

## 1.

指出下列方程的阶并判断它是线性的，还是非线性的。如果是线性的，说明它是齐次的，还是非齐次的：

(1)

$$
u_x + e^y u_y = 0;
$$

(2)

$$
u_x^2 + uu_y = 0;
$$

(3)

$$
u_t - u_{xx} + xu = 0;
$$

(4)

$$
u_{xy} -
\begin{vmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
0 & u_x & u_y \\
0 & u_{xx} & u_{yy}
\end{vmatrix}
= 0;
$$

(5)

$$
u_{xyt} -
\begin{vmatrix}
u_{xx} & u_{xy}^3 \\
0 & 2
\end{vmatrix}
= 0;
$$

(6)

$$
u_t - u_{xxt} + uu_x = 0;
$$

(7)

$$
\frac{\partial^4 u}{\partial x^4}
+ 2\frac{\partial^4 u}{\partial x^2 \partial y^2}
+ \frac{\partial^4 u}{\partial y^4}
= 0;
$$

(8)

$$
u_t + u_{xxxx} + \sqrt{1+u} = 0;
$$

(9)

$$
x^2 e^y u_{xx} + e^x u_{yy} + (x+y^2)u_{xxyy}
+ \sin(x^3+y^4)u_{xyyyy} = 0;
$$

(10)

$$
u_{xxxyy} + u_{yyyy} + 2u_x - 3u_y + \sqrt{1+u^2} = 0.
$$
